CLINICAL HISTORY: 18 year old right handed woman with recurrent seizures described as an hour of tingling and numbness in the right hand and feet.
MEDICATIONS:  Dilantin
REASON FOR STUDY:  Seizures.
INTRODUCTION:  Digital video EEG was performed using the standard 10-20 electrode placement system with additional anterior temporal and single-lead EKG electrodes.  The patient was recorded in wakefulness and sleep.  Activating procedures included hyperventilation.
TECHNICAL DIFFICULTIES:  None.

Activating procedures including hyperventilation produce no abnormal discharges.
ABNORMAL DISCHARGES:  None.
SEIZURES:  None.
IMPRESSION: Normal awake and asleep EEG.
