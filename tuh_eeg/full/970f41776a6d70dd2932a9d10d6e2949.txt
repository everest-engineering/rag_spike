REASON FOR STUDY:  Anxiety and sharp head pain.
INTRODUCTION:  Digital video routine EEG was performed using the standard 10-20 electrode placement system with additional anterior temporal and single-lead EKG electrode. TECHNICAL DIFFICULTIES:  None.

The patient is recorded in wakefulness, stage 1 and stage 2 sleep.  Activating procedures including hyperventilation produced no abnormal discharges and photic stimulation produced nice photic driving response.
SEIZURES:  None.
ABNORMAL DISCHARGES:  None.
IMPRESSION:  Normal awake and sleep EEG.
