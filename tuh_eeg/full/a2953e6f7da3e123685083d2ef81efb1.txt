INTRODUCTION
A routine EEG was performed using the standard 10120 electrode placement system with additional EKG and anterior
electrodes. Wakefulness and sleep stage 1 and stage 2 were recorded. Activation procedures including hyperventilation and photic stimulation were performed.
TECHNICAL DIFFICULTIES:
None
MEDICATIONS:
Tylenol, Seroquel, temazepam, aspirin, metformin, Remeron and simvastatin.
HISTORY:
A 43 year old female with a history of depression and hypertension, as well as diabetes,  who presents a history of seizures since age 19 with last attack In December 2010.
DESCRIPTION OF RECORD:
The record opens to a well formed posterior dominant rhythm at 9 to 10 Hz with 20 to 60 microvolts amplitude, which is
reactive to eye opening and closing. Normal frontal central beta is seen. Sleep is characterized by stage 1 and stage 2. Activation procedures
including photic stimulation and hyperventilation did not produce any abnormal discharges.
ABNORMAL DISCHARGES:
None
